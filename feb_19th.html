I am applying strategic communication to this exercise by first describing what makes a great visualization according to Ciaro and what makes a bad one.
A good visualization has the following qualities:
  1. reliable information
  2. it is functional
    • accurately depicts data, and people use the information
  3. it is aesthetically pleasing
  4. it is insightful
  5. it is enlightening


A bad visualization lacks any of these qualities.
